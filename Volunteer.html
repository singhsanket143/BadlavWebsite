Volunteer/Internship

“Volunteers don’t necessarily have time, but they have heart. As Volunteer, you will learn not just more about the needs of others, you will also learn more about your own needs and you will discover that in helping other, you help yourself most of all. A Volunteer can be anybody. There is no age limit to begin a volunteer, no preferred categories, no salary specification, no special degree or work experience. All that is required is a dedication to the cause, sincerity about the work that one in doing and commitment to a regular and sustained efforts with the organisation.”

– MAHATAMA GANDHI

BADLAV is only a medium to connect you to some hardcore realities of this country. Visit our Office to better understand the work.

Volunteering in different ways

With Your Network

Talk to 10: Beyond money and material, spread the word around in your own network of friends, family and colleagues to get more people to take some positive action. It could be about sponsoring Badlav Kits, Payroll Giving with a corporate, spreading the word about Badlav Ki Gullak, connecting to wholesalers and retail chains for unsold material/dead inventory or connecting with transporters for pro bono/ subsidized services.

Going Green

Green by Badlav is our range of up-cycled products made from last shreds of cloth. You can promote these products for conferences, workshops, camps, etc. or connect us to organizations that promote sustainable lifestyle products or organize stalls in various corporates, schools, colleges, exhibitions etc.

Working with children

Badlav’s ‘School to School’ initiative engages with urban schools, kids, teachers & parents. You can reach out to schools to organize sessions, stalls, exposure visits and collection drives for Badlav. Short supply of School material is a constant challenge at Badlav. Pitch in by organizing school material specific collection drives or connect us to stationery manufacturers.

Raise Your Hand for Women’s Dignity and Menstruation

If you are passionate about breaking the Culture of shame and silence around menstruation, get involved in this work with us.

Organising Awareness Cum Collection Camps

In January and August, wherever you are, you can organize a collection camp for Badlav, in your city. Register with Badlav and make sure to reach the collected material to the nearest Badlav dropping centre. For more details write to badlavfoundation@outlook.com

For Book Lovers

A lot of people contribute their books to Badlav. The one’s relevant for rural India are reached out to rural libraries, schools etc. while others are reached out at reasonable prices to urban masses through stalls at book fairs, college fests, PTAs in schools, etc.

Skills to share

Want to volunteer with IT, Corel draw, Photoshop, writing, videography, translation etc skills. Write to badlavfoundation@outlook.com and we will revert to you.

Disclaimer

Volunteers usually don’t get a certificate at Badlav because we believe Volunteering is about doing something selflessly. While we value your time and efforts, certificates are only issued for internship which is for 1 month at least and involves working full time with the team. Please note that contributing your surplus material is not volunteering.  If you are from a corporate/school/college/institution and want to involve your team in volunteering for Badlav, Please  write to badlavfoundation@outlook.com
