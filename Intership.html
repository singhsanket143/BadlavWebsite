Volunteer/Internship



Badlav Foundation, as the name suggests, was conceptualized on the premise of bringing ‘BADLAV’-joy to people’s lives.

The brainchild of Deepak Rawat, Badlav foundation aims at bringing about a positive change in the life of as many people as possible. Deepak Rawat a young child was driven by the extraordinary dream to see a day when no Indian child would be deprived of basic rights such as survival, protection and development.

He endeavour to do so by providing an opportunity to all those inclined to serve society and make a difference. Like many of us, he too was upset when he saw disparities between privileged & underprivileged children. He hated to see children at the age of gaining education are begging and working as servants. And then, he committed to bring a change in the lives of these children. He help in promoting education by donating books, stationery, etc. He participated in blood donation camps, done many tree plantation programme. He also provide support to children on the streets, orphans and the elderly. And focuses on empowering women and girls to achieve gender equality through equal access to various types of opportunities(health care, education, work, etc). Moreover, he also work to provide better educational facilities to trans genders and also fund scholarships for them and organized programme in which they aware them about their rights.

These qualities of resourcefulness and determination came in handy. Thus, the idea of bringing about a change saw the birth of Badlav Foundation in September 2016.

He strongly believes that there’s more to life...more than office and home and work towards the happiness and context of the less fortunate section society and believes in considering the world like our own self and come together to bring smile on everyone’s face.

This foundation is working towards helping people with their requirements and trying to bring a positive change in their lives to make it brighter.
