Transgender

Transgender people are not accepted in every society. They suffer discrimination, violence, and even murder. Transgender people have fought for and have gained many rights and protections in some societies. In many places the fight for transgender rights is associated with the fight for gay and bisexual rights. Together these groups are sometimes described by the acronym LGBT for lesbian, gay, bisexual, and transgender. Badlav providing better educational facilities to transgenders and also fund scholarships for them, make restrooms more accessible to trans peoples. Set up training in hospitals and medical schools. Badlav making bold steps to aware them about there rights if they are stopped by police and engage media coverage of transgender issues.
